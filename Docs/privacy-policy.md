# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-03

This policy applies to the IoT Panels application https://github.com/philipparndt/iot-panels

# What information that we collect

We do not provide any server and do not collect any data.

# External Servers

Within IoT Panels you can configure connections to external servers such as
MQTT brokers and InfluxDB instances. When communicating with these servers,
data may be persisted on them. Please make sure that you do not send any
private data to those servers. Please note that these external servers are
out of our responsibility.

# Third-party services

The app does not include any third-party analytics, tracking, or crash reporting libraries.

# iCloud

All settings and configurations within the app are persisted locally on your
device and will be synced to a private CloudKit database. This is done to sync
the settings between all of your iCloud devices. We do not have any access to
this data.

# Changes to the privacy policy

We reserve the right to change this privacy policy at any time. You can see all changes in this changelog:
https://github.com/philipparndt/iot-panels/commits/main/Docs/privacy-policy.md
